Our goal

We want to understand influential factors within American patients' lives of medical insurance costs and use them to create an exploratory data analysis. These are some graphs that helped us along with the creation process.

See our graphs!

Scatter Plot Graph

The graph shows that smokers have higher charges than non smokers, and that BMI has a greater effect on the charges of smokers than it does on non smokers.

Pie Charts

The first chart shows us that the data is distributed among more non smokers than smokers. However, in the second chart, the total charges for both groups are roughly equal because the higher numbers of the non smokers compensate for the higher average charges of the smokers.

Correlation Heatmaps

The upper heatmap represents the string data using One Hot Encoding, whereas the lower heatmap represents string data that was Numerically Encoded
