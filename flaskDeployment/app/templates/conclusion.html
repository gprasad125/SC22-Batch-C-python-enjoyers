After optimizing and training our models, we assessed their performance in predicting test values of our dataset. The score comparison reveals Decision Tree Regression and Polynomial Regression as the most accurate models, with Polynomial Regression slightly outperforming DTR.

Why we chose this topic

We chose this topic because healthcare is one of the top worries for Americans, as many do not have access to insurance. Our tool can serve as an aide for people to see what their insurance costs could be, and help them plan to manage these costs.

Further Extensions

If we had more time, we would try to incorporate more variables into our model, such as taking preexisting conditions into account. This could be achieved by acquiring a more complete datasets, such as directly from insurance companies.
